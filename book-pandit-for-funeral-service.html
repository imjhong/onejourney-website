Rituals Our Pandits Conduct

Pre-Cremation Rites

Leading the prayers at home, assisting with bathing rituals, and chanting during the procession to the ghat.

Cremation (Dah Sanskar)

Conducting the Mukhagni and all other essential rites at the Shamshan Ghat with Vedic mantras and reverence.

Post-Cremation (Asthi Sanchay)

Offering guidance and prayers during the collection of ashes and preparation for Asthi Visarjan.

Chautha & Tehravin

Performing the Shanti Path and other rituals during prayer meetings for the peace of the departed soul.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much notice is required to book a pandit?

+

We understand the urgency of these situations. We can typically arrange for a pandit with just a few hours' notice in most major cities. However, we recommend calling as soon as possible.

Do your pandits speak specific languages?

+

Yes, our network includes pandits proficient in various languages, including Hindi, English, Sanskrit, and several regional Indian languages. Please let us know your preference when you call.

What information do I need to provide when booking?

+

When you call, please be ready to provide the name of the deceased, the location (home/hospital), the time the service is required, and any specific community traditions you follow.

Are the charges for the pandit and the samagri included?

+

We provide a transparent quote that includes the pandit's dakshina and the cost of the complete samagri kit. There are no hidden charges.
