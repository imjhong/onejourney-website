Privacy Policy

At One Journey, we are committed to providing compassionate and respectful services. Our commitment extends to protecting your privacy and the personal information you share with us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data with the utmost care and confidentiality.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information that is necessary to provide you with our services and support. This may include:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address to communicate with you.
  • Details about your service needs, including arrangements, religious preferences, and other information required to fulfill your request.
  • Information provided through our contact form, phone calls, or WhatsApp messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose of assisting you and your family during a difficult time. We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide immediate compassionate support.
  • Coordinate and arrange funeral, memorial, and other related services as per your needs.
  • Assist with necessary documentation and legal formalities.
  • Communicate updates regarding the services and arrangements.
  • Improve our services and ensure the highest level of care.

Data Security and Confidentiality

We take the security and confidentiality of your personal information very seriously. We have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with trusted third parties, such as hospitals, government agencies, or funeral vendors, but only to the extent necessary to fulfill the services you have requested. All third-party providers are carefully vetted and are required to maintain the same level of confidentiality and data security as we do.

Your Choices and Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time. If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us using the details below.
