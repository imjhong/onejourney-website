Frequently Asked Questions

Do you serve all Christian denominations?

+

Yes, we have experience providing funeral services for all major Christian denominations, including Catholic, Protestant, Orthodox, and others. We work closely with families to ensure all specific traditions are honored.

Can you help us arrange for a priest or pastor?

+

Absolutely. We can coordinate with your family's church and clergy. If you do not have a home parish, we can help you find a priest or pastor to officiate the service.

Do you assist with booking the church and cemetery plot?

+

Yes, our services include complete coordination. We handle liaising with the church for service timings and with the cemetery administration for booking the burial plot and managing all related paperwork.

What is the difference between a casket and a coffin?

+

A coffin is typically tapered at the shoulder and has six sides, while a casket is rectangular with four sides. We can provide a catalogue with various options for both to help you choose what is most appropriate for your family.
