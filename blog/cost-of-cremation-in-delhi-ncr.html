Cost of Cremation in Delhi NCR — Updated 2025 Pricing

By One Journey Team August 8, 2025 Funeral Planning

When arranging a funeral, understanding the associated costs is a crucial step that can help families plan without unnecessary financial stress. In Delhi NCR, the cost of a cremation can vary based on several factors. This guide provides a transparent breakdown of the typical expenses you can expect in 2025.

Core Cremation Charges at Delhi's Ghats

The primary cost is the direct charge at the government or municipally-run cremation grounds (Shamshan Ghats). These fees are regulated and generally consistent.

  • CNG / Electric Cremation: This is an economical and environmentally friendly option. The charges at most MCD-run facilities in Delhi are typically in the range of ₹1,500 - ₹3,000.
  • Wood Pyre Cremation: The traditional method involves higher costs due to the price of wood and other materials. Expect the charges to be between ₹5,000 - ₹8,000, depending on the facility.
Please Note: These are estimated charges and can vary slightly between different crematoriums. It is always best to confirm the current rates.

Additional Services That Affect the Total Cost

Beyond the basic cremation charge, several other services contribute to the overall cost of the funeral ceremony.

  • Pandit / Priest Services: A knowledgeable pandit is essential for conducting the religious rites. The dakshina (offering) typically ranges from ₹2,100 - ₹5,100. You can book a pandit here.
  • Pooja Samagri: The kit of ritual items required for the ceremony can cost between ₹1,500 - ₹3,500.
  • Transportation (Hearse Van): The cost to transport the deceased depends on the vehicle type and distance, usually ranging from ₹2,500 - ₹7,000 within Delhi. Learn more about our funeral van services.
  • Ash Urn (Asthi Kalash): The price for an urn can vary from a simple earthen pot (around ₹500) to more ornate metal or biodegradable urns (₹2,000+).
