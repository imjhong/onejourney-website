Cremation Ground List in Delhi NCR with Contact Details

By One Journey Team August 8, 2025 Local Guides

During the difficult time of losing a loved one, finding information about cremation grounds (Shamshan Ghats) in Delhi NCR can be an additional source of stress. To help ease this burden, we have compiled a list of major cremation facilities across the region. Please note that booking procedures and availability can change, and it is always best to have a professional service assist you.

For immediate assistance with booking and all arrangements at any of these locations, please call One Journey at +91 9355676676.

Cremation Grounds in Delhi

South Delhi

Lodhi Crematorium

Address: Lodhi Road, Near Jawahar Lal Nehru Stadium, New Delhi

Facilities: CNG and Wood Pyre options available.

Green Park Crematorium

Address: Green Park Extension, Green Park, New Delhi

Facilities: Electric (CNG) furnaces.

North Delhi

Nigambodh Ghat

Address: Kashmere Gate, New Delhi

Facilities: Delhi's largest facility with CNG, Wood Pyre, and a dedicated section for Sikh funerals.

West Delhi

Punjabi Bagh Crematorium (Shamshan Ghat)

Address: Ring Road, Block W, Punjabi Bagh, New Delhi

Facilities: CNG and Wood Pyre options available.

East Delhi

Karkardooma / Ghazipur Crematorium

Address: Near Ghazipur Dairy Farm, Karkardooma, Delhi

Facilities: CNG and Wood Pyre options.

Cremation Grounds in Gurgaon (Gurugram)

Sector 29 Cremation Ground (Rambagh)

Address: Near Huda City Centre, Sector 29, Gurugram

Facilities: Wood Pyre and CNG options.

Cremation Grounds in Noida

Antim Niwas (Sector 94)

Address: Near Okhla Bird Sanctuary, Sector 94, Noida

Facilities: Noida's primary facility with CNG and Wood Pyre options.
