The Sacred Ritual of Ash Immersion

According to Hindu scriptures, Asthi Visarjan is the sacred ritual of immersing the ashes of the departed in a holy river. This final rite is believed to release the soul from the cycle of rebirth, helping it to achieve peace and liberation (Moksha). Our team is dedicated to helping you perform this ceremony with the utmost reverence.

Haridwar, Uttarakhand

The most preferred location from Delhi for its holy significance and proximity.

Varanasi (Kashi), UP

Known as the city of Moksha, immersion at the Ganga Ghats here holds deep spiritual importance.

Prayagraj (Allahabad), UP

Immersion at the Triveni Sangam, the confluence of three holy rivers, is considered highly auspicious.

Garh Mukteshwar, UP

A nearby and revered place on the banks of the Ganges for performing final rites.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the most preferred place for Asthi Visarjan from Delhi?

+

For families in Delhi NCR, Haridwar is the most common and preferred destination due to its deep spiritual significance and convenient proximity. We provide complete round-trip packages to Haridwar.

How long does the trip to Haridwar for Asthi Visarjan take?

+

A trip to Haridwar from Delhi NCR for the ceremony is typically a one-day affair. We plan the journey to ensure you can depart in the morning, perform the rituals peacefully, and return the same evening.

What do we need to bring with us?

+

You only need to bring the Asthi Kalash (urn). We take care of everything else, including the pandit, all the samagri for the puja, and any other arrangements at the ghat.

Are your service charges all-inclusive?

+

Yes, our quote is transparent and all-inclusive. It covers the cost of transportation (for a set number of family members), the pandit's dakshina, and all the pooja samagri. There are no hidden charges.
