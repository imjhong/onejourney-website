Questions about Prayer Meetings in Delhi

Can you book community halls or gurdwaras in Delhi for a prayer meeting?

+

Yes, absolutely. We coordinate with MCD community halls, gurdwaras managed by the DSGMC and local committees, temples, and other private venues across all parts of Delhi to find a suitable location for you.

What is included in your prayer meeting service in Delhi?

+

Our service is fully customizable but typically includes venue booking, floral and photo frame arrangements, seating, booking of artists (Bhajan/Kirtan), catering services, and AV equipment setup.

Can you arrange for specific bhajan singers or kirtan jathas?

Yes, we have a network of respected and experienced bhajan singers and Gurbani kirtan jathas in Delhi. We can make arrangements based on your specific preferences and budget.

How much advance notice is required for arrangements in Delhi?

+

We understand the time-sensitive nature of these gatherings. We can make complete arrangements within 24-48 hours. However, providing more notice allows for a better selection of available venues and artists.
