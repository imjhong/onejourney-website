Artists for a Serene & Respectful Tribute

Bhajan Singers

Arrange for experienced singers to perform soulful bhajans and devotional songs for Hindu prayer meetings like Chautha and Tehravin.

Kirtani Jathas

We coordinate with professional Kirtani Jathas to perform Gurbani Kirtan for Sikh ceremonies such as the Bhog and Antim Ardas.

Instrumentalists

For a subtle and meditative atmosphere, we can arrange for skilled instrumentalists, including harmonium, tabla, and flute players.

Ghazal Singers

For some memorial services, the reflective and soulful nature of ghazals can provide a fitting and beautiful tribute to a life lived.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much advance notice is needed to book an artist?

+

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to ensure the availability of our best artists. However, we can often accommodate more urgent requests, so please do not hesitate to call us.

Do the artists bring their own sound system?

+

Yes, our artists come fully equipped with their own instruments and a professional-grade sound system suitable for most prayer halls and community centers.

Can we request specific bhajans or shabads?

+

Absolutely. You are welcome to provide a list of your loved one's favorite devotional songs, and our artists will do their best to include them in the service.

What is the typical duration of a performance?

+

The standard duration for a prayer meeting performance is typically between 60 to 90 minutes, but this can be customized based on the schedule and flow of your ceremony.
